Artist Statement

Through my work, I like to teach the viewer. I do this by creating work that prompts the viewer to introspect. I believe when a viewer connects with a work, it will tell them more about themselves than the artist. It is important to me that my work be easily accessible and understandable, so that it can reach as many people as possible. Ultimately, I like to create work that teaches the viewer how to connect with others, their surroundings, and ultimately and most importantly, themselves.
